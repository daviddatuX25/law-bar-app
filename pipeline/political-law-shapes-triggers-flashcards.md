CARD pol-1
FRONT (shape): Arrested suspect searched without warrant, police discover drugs in pockets.
FRONT (trigger words): warrantless search, search incident to lawful arrest, pocket search
BACK (provision): Art. III Sec. 2 - Search Incident to Lawful Arrest
BACK (elements):
1. A prior valid lawful arrest has taken place
2. The search is contemporaneous with the arrest
3. The search is confined to the person of the arrestee and the area within their immediate control
BACK (common confusion): Stop and Frisk :: Stop and Frisk only requires reasonable suspicion of criminal activity and a weapon search, not a full arrest.
SOURCE: Constitution Art. III Sec. 2

CARD pol-2
FRONT (shape): Police stop motorist at routine checkpoint, order trunk opened, find contraband.
FRONT (trigger words): routine checkpoint, visual search, trunk search
BACK (provision): Art. III Sec. 2 - Routine Checkpoint Exception
BACK (elements):
1. The checkpoint is established under valid authority
2. The inspection is limited to a visual search (plain view)
3. No intrusive search (e.g. opening trunks) unless probable cause is established
BACK (common confusion): Search of Moving Vehicle :: Intrusive moving vehicle searches require independent probable cause, not just presence at a routine checkpoint.
SOURCE: Constitution Art. III Sec. 2
